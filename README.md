# ✨ Glitter List ✨

### The to-do list for people who still have their glitter pens — and use them 💖🖊️

Welcome to **Glitter List** 💅 — the to-do app that finally feels like *you*!

If your pen case has gel pens, glitter pens, and at least three shades of pink, you're in the right place. Glitter List brings that same sparkly energy to your task list. ✨📝

## 💕 Why you'll love it

- 🌸 **Gorgeous UI** — soft, sparkly, and actually fun to look at
- 📚 **Multiple lists** — Groceries. Errands. Wedding planning. Thursday. Whatever you need! Swipe left and right to flip between them 👉👈
- 🔀 **Rearrange in a flash** — long-press and drag items exactly where they belong
- ✅ **Tap to check off** — that little dopamine hit, on demand
- ✏️ **Tap to edit** — no menus, no fuss, just tap and type
- 📱 **Works offline** — no account, no sign-up, no nonsense. Your lists live on your phone.
- 🎨 **Beautiful in light and dark mode** — matches your phone, matches your vibe

## 🦋 The vibe

Glitter List is for the person who color-codes their planner 💗💛💚 and writes their shopping list in cursive. It's for anyone who thinks productivity apps are too beige and boring. ✨

Life is already serious enough. Your to-do list shouldn't be. 🌷

## 🌟 Getting started

1. 📲 Open the app
2. 📃 Tap the list icon to create your first list
3. ➕ Tap the `+` to add items
4. 🔄 Long-press and drag to reorder
5. 🎀 Swipe left or right to flip between lists

That's it. Go sparkle. ✨🌈💖
